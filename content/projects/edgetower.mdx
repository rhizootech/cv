## Context

edgE:Tower is a SaaS suite for AI-driven surveillance and operations intelligence. Born out of RCT and now under the Sentronics umbrella, it's the product I keep maintained and shipping while taking on other engagements — a working demonstration that I build *and* operate.

The platform stitches together computer vision, alerting, and operational workflows on top of camera feeds and edge devices. The "before" picture is the typical surveillance setup: hours of footage nobody watches, alerts everyone ignores, no operational follow-through. The "after" picture is *intelligence* — only events that matter, routed to the people who need to act.

## What I built

I designed and shipped the platform end-to-end: backend, frontend, edge agents, ML/Deep Learning pipelines, integrations, deployment, ongoing ops.

- **Backend** in Python — Django for the admin / management plane, FastAPI for the high-throughput API surface.
- **Computer vision** layer on OpenCV, TensorFlow, and YOLO — object detection, classification, and event extraction from live and recorded camera feeds.
- **Storage** in PostgreSQL with Redis for hot state and queues together with MongoDB for unstructured data needs.
- **Alerting** integrated to **Telegram** and **PSIMS** so operators get events on the channels they already live in + control room dashboards.
- **Cloud infrastructure** on AWS, Azure, xneelo — the usual suspects for compute, storage, and orchestration.
